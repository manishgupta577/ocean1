About Us

Mango Tree Group Ltd. is a professional maritime enterprise which provides comprehensive services in navigation route survey; waterway setting, dredging, water surface/water hyacinth clearing; ship design, building & maintenance; port/pier design, construction, operation and water transport service provision.Since its establishment in 2011, Mango Tree Group has been fully implementing the strategies as "a brand, quality, efficiency, integrity" to enable it to rapidly grow and develop with successive branches in South Sudan, Uganda, DRC, Burundi & Kenya.
